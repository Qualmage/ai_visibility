# Samsung Dashboard Demo Questions

Use these questions to test dashboard capabilities and ensure the UI can answer what the Samsung client needs.

---

## 1. Domain-Level Questions

### Single Domain Deep Dive
- What is the overall sentiment breakdown for RTINGS coverage?
- How many unique concepts does TechRadar mention about Samsung?
- What are the top negative quotes from RTINGS?
- Which prompts drive the most mentions from Tom's Guide?

### Domain Comparison
- How does RTINGS sentiment compare to TechRadar?
- Which review site has the most negative coverage?
- Which domain mentions the most unique concepts?
- Do CNET and Forbes cover similar topics or different ones?

### Domain Discovery
- Which domains mention Samsung the most?
- What are the top 10 sources for positive coverage?
- Which lesser-known domains have high negative sentiment?

---

## 2. Domain Type Questions (Owned/Earned/Competitor)

### Type Breakdown
- What percentage of mentions are from Owned vs Earned media?
- Is Earned media more positive or negative than Owned?
- How does competitor coverage sentiment compare to earned media?

### Strategic Insights
- Are we getting more positive coverage from our own properties or third parties?
- Which domain type has the most negative sentiment we need to address?
- Where should PR focus efforts - improving Owned or responding to Earned?

---

## 3. Sentiment Analysis Questions

### Sentiment Distribution
- What's the overall positive/neutral/negative split across all coverage?
- Which sentiment dominates review site coverage?
- What percentage of RTINGS mentions are negative?

### Negative Sentiment Deep Dive
- What are the top negative concepts being mentioned?
- Which quotes are most critical of Samsung products?
- What specific criticisms come from each major review site?
- Are negative mentions concentrated in specific topics or spread out?

### Positive Sentiment Analysis
- What features get the most positive coverage?
- Which products receive the most praise?
- What positive concepts should we amplify in marketing?

---

## 4. Tag/Topic Questions

### Single Topic Analysis
- What's the sentiment breakdown for "Gaming TVs"?
- How is "OLED" coverage distributed across sentiment types?
- Which domains cover "8K" the most?
- What are the key concepts within "Smart Features" coverage?

### Topic Comparison
- Is "OLED" coverage more positive than "QLED" coverage?
- Which TV feature category has the most negative sentiment?
- Do "Budget TVs" get more or less coverage than "Premium" models?
- Which topic has the widest concept diversity?

### Topic + Domain Cross-Analysis
- What does RTINGS specifically say about Gaming TVs?
- How does TechRadar cover OLED vs other sites?
- Which review site is most critical of Smart Features?
- Do competitor sites (BestBuy) focus on different topics than review sites?

### Topic Trends
- Which tags appear most frequently in prompts?
- What topics drive the most total mentions?
- Are there underrepresented topics that need more content?

---

## 5. Concept-Level Questions

### Concept Discovery
- What are the most frequently mentioned concepts?
- Which concepts have the strongest positive sentiment?
- What concepts appear in negative quotes most often?
- Which unique concepts does RTINGS mention that others don't?

### Concept + Sentiment
- Is "HDR" coverage mostly positive or negative?
- What sentiment surrounds "input lag" mentions?
- Which concepts are controversial (mixed sentiment)?
- What concepts are universally praised?

### Concept + Domain
- Which domains talk most about "burn-in"?
- Where is "Dolby Vision" mentioned most frequently?
- Do different sites emphasize different concepts?

## 6. Brand/Competitive Questions

### Brand Mentions
- How often is Samsung mentioned vs LG vs Sony?
- What sentiment surrounds Samsung brand mentions specifically?
- Which competitor gets more positive coverage on review sites?

### Competitive Positioning
- What do competitor domains (LG.com, Sony.com) say about TVs?
- How does BestBuy sentiment compare to dedicated review sites?
- Are competitor sites more neutral or do they favor their brands?

### Share of Voice
- What's Samsung's share of mentions across top review sites?
- Which brand dominates "Gaming TV" conversation?
- Who leads in positive sentiment for OLED coverage?

### Product + Feature

## 8. PR Action Item Questions

### Crisis/Response Identification
- What negative quotes need immediate response?
- Which critical reviews are from high-authority domains?
- What's the most common criticism we should address?
- Are there factual errors in negative coverage?

### Opportunity Identification
- Which positive quotes could be used as testimonials?
- What features should we emphasize based on positive coverage?
- Which review sites are most favorable for outreach?
- What topics could we create more content around?

### Media Relations
- Which journalists/sites give us the best coverage?
- Where should we focus media relationship building?
- Which sites have shifted sentiment recently?

---

## 9. Time-Based Questions

### Trend Analysis
- Has sentiment improved or declined over time?
- Are certain topics getting more coverage recently?
- How has RTINGS coverage changed month-over-month?
- When do negative mentions spike?

### Campaign Effectiveness
- Did sentiment change after product launches?
- Are PR efforts improving Earned media sentiment?
- How quickly do reviews appear after announcements?

---

## 10. Cross-Dimensional Questions (Complex Queries)

### Multi-Filter Analysis
- What negative concepts appear on RTINGS for Gaming TVs?
- Which Earned media sources have positive OLED coverage?
- What do competitor sites say negatively about specific features?
- How do Owned properties cover topics vs Earned media?

### Benchmarking
- How does our Owned sentiment compare to competitor Owned?
- Is Earned coverage more critical than competitor coverage?
- Which domain type has the most concept diversity?

### Gap Analysis
- What topics get coverage on competitor sites but not Owned?
- Which concepts appear in negative reviews that we don't address?
- Where are there content gaps between what's asked and what's covered?

## UI Capabilities Checklist

Based on these questions, the dashboard should support:

| Capability | Questions It Answers |
|------------|---------------------|
| Domain filter/selector | All domain-specific questions |
| Domain type toggle | Owned/Earned/Competitor comparisons |
| Sentiment breakdown charts | Distribution and comparison questions |
| Tag/topic filter | Topic-specific and cross-topic analysis |
| Concept word cloud or list | Concept discovery and frequency |
| Quote viewer with filters | PR action items, negative quote review |
| Comparison view (side-by-side) | Domain vs domain, topic vs topic |
| Time series charts | Trend and temporal questions |
| Drill-down capability | Going from aggregate to specific quotes |
| Export/share | Pulling data for reports |
