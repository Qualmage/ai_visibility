YOUTUBE CITATIONS: MINI-LED — EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
=================================================

TLDR: When users ask AI models about Mini-LED TVs, the #1 content format
cited back is comparison videos. Samsung needs comparison-format YouTube
content that positions Neo QLED favorably — AI models are building their
"recommended viewing" lists from this content right now.


THE NUMBERS
-----------
- 11 of 15 top-cited Mini-LED videos are direct comparisons
  (OLED vs Mini-LED, QLED vs Mini-LED)
- The top 2 prompts triggering YouTube citations are
  "Mini-LED vs QLED" (66 citations) and "Mini-LED vs OLED" (52 citations)
- Comparison queries account for ~60% of all Mini-LED YouTube citations
- Digital Trends' "OLED, QLED, or Mini-LED?" video has 2.87M views AND
  91 AI citations — comparison content earns both audience reach and AI trust


WHO AI TRUSTS
-------------
- RTINGS (111 citations) and Digital Trends (105) lead — both produce
  structured, methodical comparison content
- Small channels with focused comparison content outperform massive creators:
  ForeMost Picks (2.9K subs) beats Jon Rettinger (1.65M subs) in citations
- AI models even timestamp specific comparison moments — pointing users to
  the exact second a creator starts comparing technologies


THE TAKEAWAY
------------
Samsung needs to ensure comparison-format YouTube content positions Mini-LED
(Neo QLED) favorably against OLED and competing Mini-LED brands. AI models
are building their "recommended viewing" lists from this content, and right
now the narrative is being shaped by independent reviewers, not Samsung.
