S AI Visibility (SEMrush)

Measures how often Samsung and competitors are mentioned, cited, and the sentiment of those mentions in AI-generated responses.
Mentions
Times a brand is named in AI responses
Share of Voice (SOV)

Citations

% of links pointing to samsung.com
Sentiment
Positive, neutral, or negative tone of brand mentions

Reporting Caveats

SEMrush data is based on 383 tracked prompts across 3 AI surfaces - a representative sample from a larger prompt universe
SEMrush and Adobe track different AI platforms - direct comparison between visibility and traffic requires caution
Adobe referrer data has gaps - a significant portion of AI-driven traffic cannot be attributed to specific platforms
Google AI Overview & AI Mode traffic appears as Organic in Adobe due to referrer limitations
